TravelGo - Frontend
TravelGo's frontend is a modern and user-centric interface developed primarily with ReactJS and Bootstrap. This combination ensures an intuitive and visually appealing web experience for users. Additionally, TravelGo extends its reach to mobile users through a Flutter-based mobile application, which seamlessly integrates a web view of the TravelGo website. This approach guarantees a consistent and responsive user experience across various devices, making train booking and travel management accessible and convenient.
Source code: https://github.com/movinsilva/TravelGo-Frontend
